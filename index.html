    欢迎来到郑洋洋的主页!

    我目前是长江大学地球物理与石油资源学院数据科学与大数据技术专业大三在读学生， I am a second year PhD student in Machine Learning Department at Carnegie Mellon University , advised by Prof. Eric Xing (SAILING Lab ) and Prof. Kun Zhang (CMU-CLeaR Group ). Before that, I was an undergraduate student in Data Mining Group in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign , where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jiawei Han . For more information, please check my CV .

    My current research interests are Machine Learning , and Causality . In particular, I’m interested in data-centric explainable machine learning that utilizes multiple types of supervision with multi-modal/source data. Previously, I explored data mining, text mining and bioinformatics with an emphasis on automated knowledge construction.

    Feel free to drop me an e-mail if you are interested in my research and want to discuss relevant research topics or potential collaborations.
